Understanding the Swine Microbiome

Meta-Analysis of Core Microbiome

Our comprehensive meta-analysis defines the core sow microbiome, providing a foundation for targeted nutritional interventions.

Dietary Modulation Strategies

We develop strategies to enhance fiber digestion and improve energy extraction through postbiotic supplementation.

Predictive Modeling

Our integrated data models combine microbiome, metabolomics, and digestibility metrics to predict energy release from fibrous feeds.

Impact on Swine Production

Production Benefits

  • Improved sow health and longevity
  • Enhanced reproductive performance and lactation
  • Better piglet growth and development
  • Reduced dependency on antibiotics

Sustainability Advantages

  • Better utilization of fibrous byproducts (soybean hulls, etc.)
  • Reduced nutrient excretion and environmental impact
  • Decreased reliance on imported feed ingredients
  • Economic benefits from improved feed efficiency

Our Collaborative Approach

Dialogue Model

We engage in mutual exchange with stakeholders, creating a space for feedback and practical insights that refine our hypotheses based on real-world constraints.

Participation Model

We involve producers, nutritionists, and industry partners in co-designing trials and interpreting data to make collaborative decisions about dietary formulations.

Responsible Innovation

Our research cycle integrates adoption strategies and stakeholder feedback to continually improve our innovations and ensure they address real industry needs.
